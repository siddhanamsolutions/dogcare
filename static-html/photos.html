Photos

Profile Photo Requirements
Choose a close-up view of your face that makes it easy to identify you.
Don't use pictures of dogs for your photo.
Don't use a group photo that makes it hard to identify you.
Listing Photo Requirements (min. 3 photos)
If providing services in your home, add at least 3 photos that show:
  • Where pets will hang out.
  • Where you'll feed them.
  • Where they can sleep if you're boarding them overnight.
For all other services, add at least 3 photos of fun activities or places you take dogs.
