General Availibality

Boarding (Overnight In Your Home)

Watch pets in you home for a 24-hour period (including overnight). This also includes walks, feeding and plenty of cuddles!

Daycare (In Your Home)

Watch pets in your home while their owners are gone during the day. They won't stay overnight.

Sitting (At Clients' Home)

Stay overnight in other people's homes to watch their pets.

Dog Walking

Pick up a dog at their home for some sunshine and a walk

Home Visits

Visit people's homes to check on their pets. Visits are at least 30 minutes long and include feeding, cleaning, and other basic care.

Dog Training

Are you a professional trainer? Help dog owners correct behavioral issues (and learn new tricks) by offering training sessions.

Grooming

Are you a professional groomer? Offer hair cuts, shampoo, nail trimming, and more.

Bathing

Offer to bathe a pet in your care. You can offer this as an add-on with other servies you provide.

Pick-up / Drop-off

Pick up or drop off a pet to make things easier for owners. This can be offered as a stand-alone service or as an add-on to other services.
